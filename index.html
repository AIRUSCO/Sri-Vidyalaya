Who We Are

Sri Vidyalaya Pre-University College, Shivamogga is a well-established institution committed to delivering quality Commerce education with a strong focus on academic excellence and overall student development. As the First AI PU Commerce College in Shivamogga, we combine traditional learning with modern, technology-driven education to prepare students for the future.

Managed by Sri Vidyalaya Educational Trust, the college has built a reputation for nurturing students into confident, responsible, and career-ready individuals. We provide a disciplined and supportive learning environment where students are encouraged to achieve their full potential.

Along with academics, we emphasize personality development, communication skills, and leadership through seminars, workshops, and co-curricular activities, ensuring holistic growth.

Sri Vidyalaya PU College Courses Offered

Academic and Professional Courses

Sri Vidyalaya PU College offers specialized Commerce education designed to build a strong academic foundation and prepare students for future professional careers. Our program focuses on core subjects such as Accountancy, Economics, Business Studies, and Statistics, ensuring students gain both theoretical knowledge and practical understanding. We provide structured coaching for SEBA (Statistics, Economics, Business Studies, Accountancy) with a strong emphasis on analytical and quantitative skills, preparing students for careers in Chartered Accountancy, Economics, and Actuarial Science.

Certifications and Skill Enhancement Courses

We provide career-oriented training beyond academics to develop practical, job-ready skills essential for today’s business environment. Our programs focus on accounting practices, taxation concepts, and modern financial tools, enabling students to gain hands-on experience and build confidence. This approach helps students understand real-world applications and prepares them for careers in commerce, finance, and business sectors.
