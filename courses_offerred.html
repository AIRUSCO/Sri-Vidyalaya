Courses Offered

At Sri Vidyalaya PU College, we offer a carefully designed Commerce education that builds a strong academic foundation while preparing students for real-world success. As the First AI PU Commerce College in Shivamogga, our approach combines traditional academic excellence with modern, technology-driven learning.

We focus not only on subjects but also on skills, career readiness, and future opportunities, ensuring students are well-prepared for higher education and professional careers.

ACADEMIC and Professional Courses – SEBA and CEBA (CORE PROGRAM)

Subjects Offered:

  • • Statistics – Develops analytical thinking and data interpretation skills
  • • Computer Science - Builds digital problem-solving skills through programming, data handling, and practical technology applications for modern commerce careers.
  • • Economics – Builds understanding of financial systems and decision-making
  • • Business Studies – Provides knowledge of business operations and management
  • • Accountancy – Strengthens financial and accounting fundamentals
  • •Kannada - Strengthens language skills, cultural understanding, and effective communication in the regional context.
  • •English - Builds strong communication, comprehension, and writing skills for academic and professional success.

Key Learning Outcomes

  • Strong analytical and logical thinking ability
  • Financial literacy and accounting expertise
  • Business understanding and decision-making skills
  • Strong foundation for CA and other professional course pathways
  • Practical confidence in accounting tools like Tally and GST workflows
  • Hands-on proficiency in Excel, data handling, and digital collaboration
  • Career clarity through mentoring, seminars, and expert guidance sessions

Teaching Methodology

  • Concept-based learning for deep understanding
  • AI-enabled learning support and smart classroom environment
  • Google Classroom integration for assignments and materials
  • Weekly and monthly tests for continuous evaluation
  • Personalized mentoring and doubt-clearing sessions
  • Extra classes for academic support and improvement
  • Case-study based learning to connect commerce concepts with real business situations
